# Organic Structures (Buildings)

In *Mycelium Empire*, buildings are not constructed but *grown*. They must be planted on existing Mycelium and require Nutrients to grow.

## Core Structures

### 0. Mother Egg (HQ)
*   **Function**: The heart of the colony. Stores 500 Nutrients. Spawns initial Mycelium.
*   **Cost**: 0 Nutrients (Free, placed at game start).
*   **Limit**: 1 per game (Unique).
*   **Description**: A massive, pulsating egg shrouded in bioluminescent cyan light. The origin point of your fungal empire.
*   **Notes**: Must be placed first to start the game. Cannot be destroyed. All other buildings require existing Mycelium to be placed.

### 1. Spore Pod (Housing)
*   **Function**: Increases maximum Minion Population cap. Generates 1 Nutrient/sec.
*   **Cost**: 50 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A pulsating sack of translucent membrane. Inside, new life curls in wait.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Dense Packing*: +2 Pop Cap.
    *   *Incubator*: Minions spawn 10% faster globally.

### 2. Warrior Cyst (Barracks)
*   **Function**: Unlocks and spawns Soldier minions.
*   **Cost**: 150 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A hardened, spiked protrusion. It twitches violently when enemies are near.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Adrenal Gland*: Soldiers spawn with "Frenzy" buff.
    *   *Armored Walls*: The building itself has high HP and aggros enemies.

### 3. Nutrient Node (Storage)
*   **Function**: Increases Max Nutrient storage and provides passive generation.
*   **Cost**: 100 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A throbbing vein that roots deep into the earth, extracting trace minerals.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Deep Roots*: +1 Passive Nutrient/sec.
    *   *Overflow*: Excess nutrients heal nearby buildings.

## Defensive Structures

### 4. Acid Spitter (Turret)
*   **Function**: Static defense. Shoots acid projectiles at enemies.
*   **Cost**: 75 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A tall stalk ending in a sphincter that launches corrosive bile.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Splash Damage*: Acid hits an area.
    *   *Sticky Bile*: Slows enemies.

### 5. Wall of Flesh (Barricade)
*   **Function**: High HP blocker. Stops enemy movement.
*   **Cost**: 25 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A calcified wall of fungal matter. Nearly impenetrable.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Spiked Carapace*: Returns damage to attackers.
    *   *Regeneration*: Heals itself over time.

## Utility Structures

### 6. Pheromone Tower (Buff Beacon)
*   **Function**: Buffs nearby minions (Speed/Attack).
*   **Cost**: 200 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: Emits a visible gas that drives the colony into a frenzy.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Fear Toxin*: Scares away weak enemies.
    *   *Rally Point*: Can set a rally point for idle minions.

### 7. Nydus Tunnel (Teleporter)
*   **Function**: Instantly transports minions between two connected tunnels.
*   **Cost**: 300 Nutrients.
*   **Description**: A gaping maw that defies Euclidean geometry.
*   **Upgrades**:
    *   *Digestive Tract*: Heals minions as they travel.
