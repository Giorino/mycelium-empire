# Colony Upgrades & Mutations

This document tracks ideas for upgrades in *Mycelium Empire*. Upgrades are obtained when the colony levels up (Brotato-style).

## Tier 1: Common Mutations (Stat Boosts)
*Small, stackable improvements.*

*   **Speedy Spores**: Minion movement speed +10%.
*   **Hardened Shells**: Minion HP +15%.
*   **Sharp Mandibles**: Harvesting speed +20%.
*   **Efficient Digestion**: Nutrient gain +10%.
*   **Rapid Mitosis**: Minion spawn rate +15%.
*   **Glow Intensity**: Light radius +20% (reveals more map).
*   **Sticky Secretion**: Enemy movement speed -10% on Mycelium.

## Tier 2: Rare Mutations (New Mechanics)
*Significant changes that alter gameplay.*

*   **Acidic Trail**: Mycelium deals 5 DPS to enemies walking on it.
*   **Spore Cloud**: On death, minions release a cloud that poisons enemies.
*   **Hive Mind**: All minions gain +1 Attack for every 10 minions alive.
*   **Regenerative Tissue**: Minions heal 1 HP per second while on Mycelium.
*   **Nutrient Recycling**: 25% chance to recover cost when a minion dies.
*   **Wall Breaker**: Minions deal 2x damage to rock walls.

## Tier 3: Legendary Mutations (Synergies)
*Game-defining powers that enable specific builds.*

*   **Explosive Decompression**: Minions explode for 50 damage on death. (Synergy with *Rapid Mitosis*)
*   **Vampiric Fungi**: Minions heal for 20% of damage dealt. (Synergy with *Hardened Shells*)
*   **Parasitic Infection**: Enemies killed on Mycelium have a 20% chance to spawn a friendly minion.
*   **Photosynthesis**: Passively gain 1 Nutrient per second for every 10 Mycelium tiles.
*   **Titan Strain**: Minion max population -50%, but all minions have 3x stats.
